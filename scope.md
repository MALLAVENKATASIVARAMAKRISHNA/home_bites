## Scope of the Project

- Develop a web-based application for a home-based food business  
- Enable customers to view available home-made food items  
- Allow customers to place food orders online  
- Store customer and order details securely in a database  
- Provide an admin system to manage food items and prices  
- Track and update order status (pending, prepared, delivered)  
- Support order-based food supply (no shop or restaurant model)  
- Simplify order management and reduce manual errors
